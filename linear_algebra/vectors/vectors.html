1. Scalar multiplication

\vec{u} = \left(\begin{matrix} 3 \\ 7 \\ 4 \\ 2 \end{matrix}\right) \, What \, is \,\, 5 \cdot \vec{u} = ?

Solution:
\\[3mm] \left(\begin{matrix} 5 \cdot 3 \\ 5 \cdot 7 \\ 5 \cdot 4 \\ 5 \cdot 2 \end{matrix}\right)

Answer:
\\[3mm] \left(\begin{matrix} 15 \\ 35 \\ 20 \\ 10 \end{matrix}\right)


2.

\left(\begin{matrix} 5 \\ 9 \end{matrix}\right) + \left(\begin{matrix} 1 \\ 7 \end{matrix}\right) = ?

Solution:
\\[3mm] \left(\begin{matrix} 5 + 1 \\ 1 + 7 \end{matrix}\right)

Answer:
\\[3mm] \left(\begin{matrix} 7 \\ 16 \end{matrix}\right)
